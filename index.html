Penn Hills Cinema

Originally opened in 1966 The Penn Hills Cinemas is one of the last remaining independently owned and operated multiplexes in The Pittsburgh Area. It is owned and operated by Paul T. Looker. It is our goal to provide you with the best movie going experience possible while keeping the costs down. If you can find a first run theater anywhere around with better prices we won't just match them we will beat them. Guaranteed.

Open 365 days a year.

Hours of operation:
